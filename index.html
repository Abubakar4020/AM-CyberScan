About AM CyberScan

AM CyberScan is a comprehensive security tool designed to protect users from phishing attacks and modern cyber threats. It carefully scans both URLs and uploaded files to detect malicious links, hidden malware, and suspicious indicators. By analyzing potential risks in real time, the system determines whether content is safe or unsafe. With fast, reliable, and intelligent detection, AM CyberScan helps users stay secure and confident online.

Features:

  • URL & Email Content Scanning
  • File Malware Detection
  • Real-time Risk Assessment
  • PDF Report Export
  • Dashboard Analytics
  • Scan History Reports
